# Pass without Trace
*2nd-level, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V, S, M (ashes from burned mistletoe)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

You radiate a concealing aura in a 30-foot [Emanation](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) for the duration. While in the aura, you and each creature you choose have a +10 bonus to Dexterity ([Stealth](skills.md#Stealth)) checks and leave no tracks.
