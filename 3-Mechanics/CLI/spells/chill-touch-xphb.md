# Chill Touch
*cantrip, Necromancy*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Channeling the chill of the grave, make a melee spell attack against a target within reach. On a hit, the target takes `dice:1d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`d10`) Necrotic damage, and it can't regain [Hit Points](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) until the end of your next turn.

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by `dice:1d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`d10`) when you reach levels 5 (`dice:2d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d10`)), 11 (`dice:3d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`3d10`)), and 17 (`dice:4d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d10`)).
