# Boots of Elvenkind
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  
![](/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/img/boots-of-elvenkind.webp#right)


While you wear these boots, your steps make no sound, regardless of the surface you are moving across. You also have [Advantage](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on Dexterity ([Stealth](skills.md#Stealth)) checks.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 239. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
