# Lock
*Adventuring gear*  


- **Cost**: 10 gp
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

A Lock comes with a key. Without the key, a creature can use [Thieves' Tools](/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/thieves-tools-xphb.md) to pick this Lock with a successful DC 15 Dexterity ([Sleight of Hand](skills.md#Sleight%20of%20Hand)) check.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 226. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
